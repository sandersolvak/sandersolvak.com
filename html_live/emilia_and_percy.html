Emilia & Percy

“Emilia and Percy” is a lovely childrens book written by Liz Bowen. It follows Emilia and her toy penguin Percy as they set out to help a squirrel in the middle of the winter in New York's Central Park. I did the overall design, art direction and layout, prepress work as well as the ebook conversion. It's illustrated by the talented Kristi Kindel.

30×30cm, 48 pages, typeset in Gordon Black and Chaparral.

You can buy it as a 30×30cm hardcover book at Blurb.com or as a 22×22cm paperback or a Kindle ebook on Amazon.
