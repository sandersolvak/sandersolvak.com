Identity for carfits.me

Carfits.me is a young startup aiming to take the guesswork out of buying the right car. Asking some simple questions and applying some clever magic, carfits.me will try and help you find the car that's a best fit for your needs.

The logo emerged from the idea of having a perfect car on your mind but not being able to quite put it into words. Carfits.me can help you there.
