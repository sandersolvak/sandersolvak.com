Näidud.ee website

Näidud.ee lets individuals or apartment associations report all utility meter readings from a single place. Founded by a couple of guys fed up with the hassle that is reporting monthly meter readings to service providers, näidud.ee lets you send and collect readings, quick and easy.

The site allows you to set up service providers you need to report to, configure reminders, delegate reporting, see detailed graphical history reports and has many more useful features.
